During my internship as a Cloud Engineer Intern at AWS from April 17, 2026 to July 10, 2026, I had the opportunity to apply the knowledge gained from university to a professional cloud computing environment while expanding my practical experience with Amazon Web Services (AWS). Throughout the internship, I learned and worked with a variety of AWS services, including IAM, EC2, S3, RDS, VPC, Elastic Load Balancer, Auto Scaling, CloudWatch, Lambda, and AWS CLI.

In addition to studying AWS technologies, I participated in the analysis and cloud architecture design of the PharmaCare AI project. My responsibilities included analyzing system requirements, designing cloud infrastructure, configuring AWS resources, deploying cloud services, and preparing technical documentation. Through these activities, I strengthened my skills in cloud computing, system analysis, infrastructure deployment, technical documentation, problem-solving, teamwork, and professional communication.

Throughout the internship, I maintained a responsible and proactive attitude toward my assigned tasks.
